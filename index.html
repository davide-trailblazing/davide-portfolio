My coaching method, across the venture lifecycle

The method delivers strategic support to each founder by building the venture's growth roadmap across the investment lifecycle and planning milestone-based fundraising rounds that prove the return on investment that venture capitalists look for, and it guides each startup through the critical maturity phases: customer discovery to achieve problem-solution fit (Customer Persona), sales discovery to map the buying decision (Customer Buying Decision), repeatable go-to-market actions to validate product-market fit (Customer Journey), and scaling a proven business model and its underlying infrastructure (Customer Economics). At every step I connect founders, through a warm first introduction, to the industry leaders, venture experts, institutional partners, customers, and investors they need to grow.

1Problem-Solution Fit
Customer discovery, "don't sell, just ask."
2Product-Market Fit
Business model, OKRs, cash-flow management.
3Go-to-Market
Pipeline systems, repeatable demand-supply.
4Fundraising
Milestones, comparables, ROI storytelling.
What each stage covers
1 Customer Persona
Problem-Solution Fit
Customer-discovery interviews, Mom Test framing, Pain Funnel, early-adopter ID, riskiest-assumption testing, problem-solution canvas.
2 Customer Buying Decision
Product-Market Fit
Learn-Build-Measure loops, minimum-viable-product iteration, willingness-to-pay tests, retention/expansion signals, quarterly objectives tied to a single North-Star metric.
3 Customer Journey
Go-to-Market Scaling
Ideal-customer-profile and persona definition, pipeline construction, structured sales-qualification methods (MEDDPIC, SPIN), return-on-investment calculators, demand staging, customer-journey mapping.
4 Customer Economics
Fundraising
Comparables, milestones-to-funds plans, dilutive vs. non-dilutive (federal/state incentive/strategic), data room & investor storytelling.

Program Cornerstones for Founders

Every founder I coach works toward three cornerstones. Each one produces concrete, tangible deliverables they keep and can share across the venture ecosystem.

CORNERSTONE 01 · PLAN FORWARD
Talk the walk: a clear 3 to 5 year story
Sharpen how the business is communicated to investors (return on investment) and to customers (value proposition and product).
Overview for Press Release Executive Summary for Investors Sales Collateral for Customers Pitch Deck Cash flow, financing & milestones
CORNERSTONE 02 · EXECUTE WEEKLY
Walk the talk: win 5 stakeholders a week
Over three months, reach 5 industry stakeholders a week: customers (customer discovery, problem-solution fit) and revenue (sales discovery, product-market fit).
OKRs & ICPs (root hypothesis) Weekly Sales Priorities (with blockers) Bespoke sales: do things that don't scale Customer pipeline: the scalable engine
CORNERSTONE 03 · CONNECT TO THE ECOSYSTEM
Care and share: build the network
I introduce 5 venture experts a week, for fast feedback now and lasting mentors, funders, customers, and partners over time.
Wishlist sheet for Mentors & Investors Expert sessions: 5 a week (weeks 1 to 6) Investors & partners days (25, week 7) Accountability newsletter (highlights & lowlights)
