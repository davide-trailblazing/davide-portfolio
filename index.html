My Founders Scaling Steps, by Stage

The coaching itself is a step-by-step program I built around the customer. Founders move from problem-solution fit through customer discovery (“don’t sell, just ask”), to product-market fit via business-model design, OKRs, and cash-flow management, to a repeatable go-to-market built on pipeline systems and demand-supply case studies, and finally to investor-ready fundraising with milestone planning, comparables, and return-on-investment storytelling, delivered as weekly, personalized strategy sessions. It’s the same end-to-end view of a business a banker needs to judge what’s fundable and where a company will struggle.

Stage 01 · Customer Persona
Problem-Solution Fit
Customer-discovery interviews, Mom Test framing, Pain Funnel, early-adopter ID, riskiest-assumption testing, problem-solution canvas.
Stage 02 · Customer Buying Decision
Product-Market Fit
Learn-Build-Measure loops, minimum-viable-product iteration, willingness-to-pay tests, retention/expansion signals, quarterly objectives tied to a single North-Star metric.
Stage 03 · Customer Journey
Go-to-Market Scaling
Ideal-customer-profile and persona definition, pipeline construction, structured sales-qualification methods (MEDDPIC, SPIN), return-on-investment calculators, demand staging, customer-journey mapping.
Stage 04 · Customer Economics
Fundraising
Comparables, milestones-to-funds plans, dilutive vs. non-dilutive (federal/state incentive/strategic), data room & investor storytelling.
Founders Sales Foundations · Weekly Sales Challenge
ICP & Persona
Ideal-Customer-Profile worksheets, day-in-the-life personas, buying-committee mapping.
Customer Discovery
Mom Test interview script, Pain Funnel, jobs-to-be-done framing, surfaces real pain, not vanity validation.
Sales Discovery
MEDDPIC + SPIN qualification, ROI calculators, objection-handling library, used in the gener8tor 7-week sales challenge.
Customer Journey
End-to-end journey map (awareness → expansion), conversion-rate review, churn-cause taxonomy.
OKRs & North Star
Quarterly OKRs tied to one North-Star metric, leading vs. lagging indicators, weekly review cadence.
Investor Storytelling
Narrative arc (problem → wedge → why-now → traction → ask), data-room build, 60+ curated investor intros per founder.
